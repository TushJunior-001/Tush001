07 — FAQ

Investor Questions

Answers to the questions serious investors ask first. If yours is not here, contact directly.

Are your results audited or verified? +
Performance data is pulled directly from prop firm platform dashboards (FTMO, Topstep) and TradeZella analytics which auto-syncs from broker accounts. These are not self-reported spreadsheets. Full trade-by-trade exports, monthly statements, and payout certificates are provided under NDA for due diligence purposes. Third-party audit via an independent CPA is available at investor request for any partnership above a certain threshold.
What is your approach to drawdown and risk? +
Capital protection is the primary objective. Maximum risk per trade is 1% of account. A hard daily loss limit of 3% triggers a complete stop for the day regardless of conviction. The maximum historical drawdown across all accounts is 4.8% — significantly below the industry norm. I trade with the same risk discipline on prop firm accounts as I would on personal capital.
Can you describe your strategy without giving away your edge? +
The approach is macro-driven and systematic. Directional bias is established from fundamental economic and geopolitical analysis before any chart is opened. Multiple independent confirmation conditions must align before a position is entered. Both long-term trend following and shorter-term countertrend opportunities are taken depending on market structure. The complete methodology documentation is included in the due diligence package under NDA.
What markets and instruments do you trade? +
Primary focus is major and minor Forex pairs, Gold, Silver, WTI and Brent crude oil, and equity index futures (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow). Each instrument is traded with its specific fundamental drivers in mind — macroeconomic data for currencies, supply dynamics for commodities, risk regime for indices. Correlated instruments are never counted as separate positions for risk purposes.
Are you open to managing external capital? +
Yes, this is the objective. I am currently building the verifiable track record required for institutional conversations. For strategic investors, family offices, or qualified individuals interested in managed account arrangements, I welcome a conversation. All enquiries are handled with full confidentiality under mutual NDA. Contact to discuss structure, terms, and minimum commitment.
Why Kenya — does location affect execution? +
Location does not affect trading execution. All trading is conducted digitally through regulated prop firm platforms with execution handled by their global infrastructure. Analysis and trade management operate 24 hours during market hours. East Africa Time (EAT, UTC+3) provides excellent coverage of both the London and New York sessions — the two most liquid periods in global markets — without the late-night hours that affect traders in Asia-Pacific.
